**BlanketBot Privacy Policy**

**Last updated:** July 18, 2026

BlanketBot is a Discord moderation and server-management application operated by Sal & Associates.

This policy explains what information BlanketBot receives, how that information is used, how long it may be retained, and how users or server administrators may request deletion.

**Information We Collect**
The bot only collects and stores information necessary to provide its moderation and server management features. This may include Discord server IDs, channel IDs, user IDs, role IDs, moderation logs, configuration settings, and audit information related to moderation actions. We do not collect personal information beyond what is provided by the Discord platform.

**Discord Data**
The bot interacts with Discord through the official Discord API. BlanketBot uses the Message Content Intent to process message content only when necessary to provide moderation features such as filtering, automoderation, logging, and command handling. BlanketBot also uses the Server Members Intent to identify members, apply moderation actions, manage roles, and provide server management features. Any information received from Discord is used solely to provide moderation, logging, and server management features. Discord has its own Privacy Policy that governs how it handles your account and personal information.

**Data Storage**
Configuration data, moderation records, and other operational records are stored only for as long as needed to operate the bot's features. Configuration and moderation data may be retained after the bot is removed from a server until it is deleted through routine maintenance or following a valid deletion request. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties except where required by law.

**Third-Party Services**
BlanketBot may use third-party hosting providers, cloud infrastructure, databases, monitoring services, or error reporting services to operate the bot. These providers may process limited technical information necessary to provide reliable service. These providers maintain their own privacy policies.

**Support Requests**
If you contact us for support through Discord, email, or another platform, we will retain your contact information and any details you provide only for the purpose of responding to your request. We do not use this information for marketing purposes or share it with third parties.

**Security**
We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored by the bot and to maintain the security and integrity of our systems. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to minimize risk wherever possible.

**Bot Usage**
Server administrators are responsible for configuring and using the bot in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. BlanketBot may send edited or deleted message content to a server-configured Discord moderation log channel. These Discord messages are retained according to the server's configuration and Discord's own data handling practices. Users should be aware that moderation actions, including message edits, message deletions, warnings, mutes, bans, and other administrative actions performed by the bot, may be logged for administrative purposes.

**Data Deletion Requests**
Server administrators may request deletion of stored server configuration and moderation records. Individual users may also contact us regarding personal data associated with their Discord account. Requests will be reviewed and processed within a reasonable period, subject to identity verification and any legal, security, fraud prevention, or abuse prevention obligations.

**Changes to This Policy**
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with a revised update date.
